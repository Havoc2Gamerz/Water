Water Conservation

What is Water Conservation

Water conservation is using water efficiently to reduce waste of water and overuse of water. Preservation and control of water can have many benefits.

Effects of Water Conservation

Effects of not conserving water are, pollution, poor water quality, drought, more cost, waste more energy,
