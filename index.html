Water Conservation

What is Water Conservation

Water conservation is using water efficiently to reduce waste of water and overuse of water. Making the decision to conserve water will have an impact on many problems. Preservation and control of water will help improve the quality of water, pollution, save energy, droughts and many more.

Effects of Water Conservation

Effects of not conserving water are, water pollution, poor water quality, drought, more cost, waste more energy,

There are many ways to conserve water. You can help conserve water.
