Check Faucets and Pipes for Leaks

Checking for leaks will have a large impact on water conservation. Small drops of water can waste about 20 gallons of water per day. Larger leaks can be more up to hundreds of gallons. Checking for leaks will conserve water and will save you a lot of money on the water bill.

Turning off the water when not using it

When you're brushing teeth or shaving or washing your car make sure to turn off the water. Doing that will conserve lots of water. Bathing

When you shower you can use up to 20 to 40 gallons of water. Turning off the water when soaping up can save a lot of water. Also taking more baths can save more water than showering everyday.
